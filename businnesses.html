BUSINESSES IN DHARAVI

Dharavi is home to a burgeoning economy with $1 billion turnover annually, powered by 20,000 factories and 5,000 businesses. The industries Dharav's entrepeneurs are most active in are leather, pottery, and handicrafts. Click on the videos below to learn more about Dharavi's economy and businesses.

SUPPORT DHARAVI's BUSINESSES

The area where Dharavi's businesses most need help is in getting access to more consumers, since they are not often able to distribute their products where consumers shop. However, techonology is helping solve that proble. The ecommerce site Dharavimarket focuses on making available products made by Dharavi artisans to consumers across India, and the world. Watch the video below to learn more about them and click on their logo to visit dharavimarket.com to support businesses in Dharavi by buying their products, and sharing recommendations with your friends and family.
